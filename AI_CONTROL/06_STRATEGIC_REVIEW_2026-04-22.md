# Strategic Review — 2026-04-22

## Purpose

This document summarises the final conclusions from the external AI review process for Unitas-GridFlow.

It is intended to capture the important conclusions without bringing raw external review materials into the live repo.

---

## What the review process confirmed

- The project addresses a real workflow problem.
- The narrow pre-CAD QA gatekeeper framing is correct.
- The project is strongest as an internal tool or consultancy leverage asset.
- The current MVP is credible and worth continuing.

---

## What the review process challenged

- The project still lacks real-world validation using real survey files and real users.
- The current rules are meaningful for an MVP but are not yet deeply differentiated or truly DNO-grade.
- There is a risk of continuing to build a polished but unvalidated prototype.

---

## Most important conclusion

The next phase should be validation-led, not feature-led.

The central unresolved question is:

**Does the current tool provide meaningful value on a real survey file for a real user?**

---

## Best current framing

Best current framing:

- internal tool
- consultancy leverage asset
- workflow productivity tool

Less realistic current framing:

- standalone commercial product
- broad SaaS platform
- major utility software business at this stage

---

## Development implication

The next stage should prioritise:

- obtaining one or more real survey files
- testing the current tool against them
- observing what breaks, what matters, and what real users care about
- refining the roadmap based on that evidence

---

## What should not be prioritised yet

Do not prioritise:

- broad commercial packaging
- platform expansion
- major feature growth without validation
- cosmetic roadmap work detached from real usage

---

## Final decision

Continue with modifications.

This means:

- continue the project
- keep it narrow
- keep the codebase healthy
- but shift the next phase toward real-world proof

---

## Status impact

This review does not invalidate the current project.

It changes the emphasis of the next phase from:

- feature expansion
  to:
- validation and proof-of-value
