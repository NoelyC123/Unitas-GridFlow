About

Unitas GridFlow validates surveyed pole data against DNO-oriented QA checks before it reaches CAD design.

Current capabilities: CSV upload, schema normalization, QA issue detection, map review, PDF reporting, and job browsing.

Current build: working local MVP with active CI, linting, and initial pytest coverage.
