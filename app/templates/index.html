About

Unitas GridFlow is a survey-to-design handoff layer for overhead line work. It turns raw controller exports into project-based review evidence before design work starts.

Current capabilities: project intake, multi-file survey grouping, QA and design-readiness checks, route sequencing, EXpole pairing review, map/PDF review, and D2D handoff exports.

Current build: Stage 3 controlled remote intake validated with Cloudflare Access and real Gordon survey data.
