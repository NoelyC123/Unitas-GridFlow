# DNO Data Request - Overhead Line Survey Data

This template is intended for designers or project managers requesting engineering data from a Distribution Network Operator. Remove sections that do not apply before sending.

Project Reference: Enter the ICP or contractor project reference.

Site Location: Enter the route, road names, site name, or general location.

DNO Licence Area: ENWL / NGED / SPEN / SSEN / UKPN / NPG.

Requesting Organisation: Enter company name.

Contact: Enter name, role, email, and phone number.

Date of Request: Enter date.

Required By: Enter required response date, if applicable.

## Purpose of Request

This request is submitted in support of an overhead line infrastructure survey conducted as part of ICP connection, reinforcement, diversion, or asset alteration works.

Field survey evidence has been captured and correlated to DNO baseline records using the GridFlow survey-to-design workflow. The following engineering data is required before design work can be completed.

GridFlow has identified the support numbers and verification items listed below. Field observations are supplied as supporting evidence only. Certified engineering data remains required from the DNO.

## Baseline Reference

Survey conducted against: State the baseline source, such as ENWL Network Asset Viewer, DNO GIS export, Trimble export, or DNO-provided schedule.

Survey date or dates: Enter survey dates.

Poles surveyed: Enter count.

Support numbers covered: List support numbers here or attach the GridFlow per-pole summary CSV.

GridFlow run reference: Enter run name or report identifier if available.

## Data Required

### 1. Voltage Confirmation

Required for: Enter number of poles.

Support numbers: List affected support numbers.

Data required: Certified operating voltage for each listed pole and associated spans.

Why needed: Voltage is not reliably determinable from field observation alone. Warning signs may be absent, unclear, outdated, or not visible from the survey position.

### 2. Conductor Specification

Required for: Enter number of poles or spans.

Support numbers or spans: List affected support numbers, spans, or route sections.

Data required:

- Conductor size in square millimetres, where available.
- Conductor type, such as AAC, ACSR, copper, covered conductor, or ABC.
- Conductor material and configuration.

Why needed: Conductor data is not reliably field-observable and is required for loading, clearance, and design calculations.

### 3. Pole Class / Strength Rating

Required for: Enter number of poles.

Support numbers: List affected support numbers.

Data required: Pole class, strength rating, manufactured height, or equivalent structural rating held by the DNO.

Why needed: Pole class and strength are required for structural assessment, stay design, retention assessment, and loading calculations. They cannot be confirmed from normal field photos.

### 4. Identity Confirmation

Required for: Enter number of poles.

Support numbers: List affected support numbers.

Issue: GridFlow matched field evidence at MEDIUM or LOW confidence, or the support number was not directly visible in field evidence.

Data required: Confirmation that each support number corresponds to the physical pole at the surveyed location.

Why needed: Design should not proceed on an uncertain identity match.

### 5. Re-inspection Request

Required for: Enter number of poles.

Support numbers: List affected support numbers.

Observed issue: Summarise visible defects or uncertainty, such as rot, damage, lean, split timber, vegetation obstruction, restricted inspection access, or unclear base condition.

Data requested:

- Current DNO inspection assessment.
- Latest inspection date.
- Inspection outcome.
- CNAIM health index or equivalent condition category, if available.
- Any DNO instruction on retention, replacement, or further inspection.

Why needed: Field photos indicate a possible condition issue that should be assessed against DNO inspection records before design.

### 6. Asset History and Equipment Data

Required for: Enter support numbers where relevant.

Data requested:

- Installation date, if available.
- Last inspection date.
- Transformer rating, if applicable.
- Switchgear, cutout, recloser, or other pole-mounted equipment details.
- Ownership or joint-use status, if relevant.

Why needed: Equipment ratings, ownership, and asset history may affect design scope, responsibilities, and safety review.

## Attachments

Attach the following where available:

- GridFlow QA report.
- Per-pole evidence summary CSV.
- Baseline match register.
- Field photo evidence, if approved for sharing.
- Map popup screenshots, if approved for sharing.
- Route plan or project drawing, if available.

## Response Format Requested

Preferred response format: CSV or spreadsheet with one row per support number.

Acceptable response format: PDF schedule, formal data pack, or DNO engineering report.

Requested columns, where available:

- Support number.
- Pole identifier or asset ID.
- Coordinates.
- Voltage.
- Conductor size, type, and material.
- Pole class or strength rating.
- Pole height.
- Equipment details and ratings.
- Last inspection date.
- Inspection outcome or condition category.
- Notes or restrictions.

## Notes

All field evidence has been captured for survey-to-design workflow support. It should not be treated as certified engineering data.

GridFlow identifies the data needed for design review. Final engineering decisions remain subject to DNO records, designer assessment, statutory requirements, and project-specific approvals.
