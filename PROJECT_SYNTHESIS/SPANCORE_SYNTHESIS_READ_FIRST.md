# SpanCore Synthesis — Read First

## Purpose
This folder contains the rebuilt synthesis pack for the SpanCore / EW Design Tool project.

It consolidates:
- the four raw AI analyses
- the cross-AI comparison matrix
- the issue-by-issue claims ledger
- the final master synthesis
- the decision memo
- and the execution alignment plan

The purpose of this pack is to provide a clean, durable record of the strongest current judgment about:
- what SpanCore is
- whether it is worth continuing
- what its best realistic form is
- and what should happen next

### 1. Read the final synthesis first
